Collaboration & Partnership

Partners

We are actively building relationships with mission-aligned humanitarian NGOs.

The Value We Bring

Why Partner With GCHP?

GCHP connects humanitarian NGOs with a motivated, coordinated student network. Here is what that means for your organization.

Access to Student Networks

Reach motivated student leaders and organizations across campuses in the US and internationally — a ready audience that cares about your mission.

Expanded Awareness

Bring your mission into student spaces through organized events, campaigns, and educational outreach — sustained across an entire academic year.

Mission-Aligned Collaboration

Partner with an organization focused on mutual value, long-term relationship-building, and measurable support — not one-off transactions.

Sustainable Fundraising

Build more sustainable student-led fundraising pipelines instead of isolated one-time efforts — recurring, scalable, and accountable.

What We Offer

Partnership Opportunities

We work with NGO partners to deploy the resources and networks of a motivated student movement in service of your mission. Here is what that looks like in practice.

Student-Led Fundraising Campaigns

Organized, synchronized campaigns across multiple campuses with centralized tracking and transparent reporting to your organization.

Awareness & Advocacy Events

Ambassador-organized events on campus that educate students about your mission, the issues you address, and the impact of your work.

Educational Programming & Speaker Sessions

Opportunities for NGO representatives to speak directly to student communities, building deeper understanding and authentic connection.

Social Media & Digital Campaigns

Coordinated digital outreach amplifying your mission across student social networks, with consistent messaging and dignified storytelling.

Long-Term Campus Development

Building a sustained student presence for your mission across campuses — not just one event, but a growing, year-round relationship.

Our Partner Standards

"We work exclusively with established, vetted humanitarian NGOs operating in conflict-affected regions. One NGO partner per campaign. Transparent reporting required. No political organizations."

Who We Partner With

  • Established humanitarian NGOs with verified field presence
  • Organizations operating in conflict-affected regions
  • Partners with transparent accountability structures
  • Mission-aligned groups committed to long-term collaboration
  • Organizations able to provide impact verification and reporting
Our Philosophy

Not Transactional. Not Short-Term.

We build relationships that grow over multiple campaigns and cohorts. We amplify what NGOs are already doing — we don't ask partners to change their programs to fit us.

Our goal is to become a reliable, scalable student-facing arm for NGO partners who want to build deeper connections with the next generation of global health advocates. We grow together.

Local → Global

Campaigns start campus-level but scale across a coordinated international student network simultaneously.

Amplify, Not Replace

We amplify what you're already doing. Your programs, your expertise, your relationships on the ground.

Verified Impact

We require transparent reporting from partners so we can honestly communicate impact to the students who gave.
