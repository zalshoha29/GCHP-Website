Launching Fall 2026

The 30 University Campaign

In Fall 2026, GCHP will launch its first coordinated international student campaign across 30 universities — uniting students from campuses across North America and beyond under one shared mission, one vetted NGO partner, and one measurable humanitarian goal.

This is what student-led humanitarian action looks like when it's coordinated, not fragmented. When it's sustained, not one-off. When impact is measured, not assumed.

How It Works

One Campaign. 30 Campuses.

Every participating university runs the same campaign at the same time — creating a synchronized, high-impact wave of student action.

Unified Campaign

All 30 universities run synchronized events, fundraising drives, and awareness campaigns during the same window — amplifying collective reach and impact.

One Vetted NGO Partner

All fundraising flows through a single, rigorously vetted humanitarian organization operating in conflict-affected regions — ensuring accountability and transparency.

Measurable Outcomes

Every dollar raised is mapped to a specific, partner-verified humanitarian outcome. Impact reports are published publicly after the campaign closes.

Our Commitment

Transparency in Everything

All fundraising is mapped to specific humanitarian outcomes through vetted NGO partners. We publish partner-verified reports. We don't inflate impact numbers. We don't count awareness as humanitarian outcomes.
